monkey d.luffy

Monkey D.luffy is the energetic, rubber-bodied pirate captain and protagonist of the One Piece manga/anime
, known as "Straw Hat" Luffy, whose dream is to find the legendary treasure One Piece and become the Pirate King
, symbolizing ultimate freedom, achieving this by eating the Gum-Gum (Human-Human Fruit, Model: Nika)
Devil Fruit, gaining rubber powers, and leading his diverse crew, the Straw Hat Pirates, on epic adventures.
